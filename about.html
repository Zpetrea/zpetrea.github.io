Our team brings decades of passion and precision to every outdoor space we touch. We’re dedicated to quality and customer satisfaction across Western North Carolina.

About Our Mission

We are a professional landscaping company that has provided residential and commercial services for the Western region of North Carolina for over 35 years.

Our team combines decades of experience with a passion for transforming outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional landscapes. We believe in quality craftsmanship, transparent communication, and customer satisfaction. Established in Granite Falls, North Carolina, Clean Cutters Landscaping has proudly served residential and commercial clients for over 30 years. Rooted in tradition and driven by excellence, our locally owned and operated business was built on the belief that a well-maintained landscape adds beauty, value, and peace of mind to every property. What began as a small operation in the heart of Granite Falls has evolved into a trusted name across western North Carolina, recognized for its reliable service, expert craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

At Clean Cutters Landscaping, we specialize in a full range of landscaping and grounds maintenance services tailored to meet the unique needs of both homeowners and businesses. From lawn care, tree and shrub management, seasonal cleanups, and mulch installation to full-scale design and hardscaping projects, we bring passion and precision to every job—big or small. Our team comprises seasoned professionals who bring decades of hands-on experience and a deep understanding of the regional climate and terrain. Whether you're looking to enhance curb appeal, maintain a safe and inviting commercial exterior, or create your dream outdoor living space, Clean Cutters Landscaping delivers dependable results and personalized service with every visit. As a long-standing part of the Granite Falls community, we value relationships just as much as results. That’s why we continue to grow primarily through word-of-mouth and repeat business—proof of the trust we've earned from generations of satisfied customers. Clean Cutters Landscaping—over 30 years of quality, care, and commitment you can see.
